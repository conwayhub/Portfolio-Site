Conway gazed up at the enormous face. Forty years it had taken them to learn what kind of smile was hidden beneath the dark moustache. O cruel, needless misunderstanding! O stubborn, self-willed exile from the loving breast! Two gin-scented tears trickled down the sides of their nose. But it was all right, everything was all right, the struggle was finished. They had won the victory over himself.

They loved customer service.

To die hating them, that was freedom.
